Discover AlUla

AlUla is one of Saudi Arabia's most breathtaking travel destinations. Located in the northwest of the Kingdom, this historic region offers a unique blend of ancient heritage, dramatic desert landscapes, and remarkable cultural experiences.

For thousands of years, AlUla served as an important stop along the ancient incense trade route. Today, visitors from around the world come to explore its archaeological treasures, natural beauty, and vibrant tourism activities.

Experience the Beauty of AlUla

The stunning rock formations and golden desert scenery make AlUla one of the most photogenic places in the Middle East.
