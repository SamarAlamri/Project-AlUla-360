Tourism Services in AlUla

AlUla offers a wide range of tourism services designed to help visitors explore the region comfortably and safely. These services allow tourists to experience the historical, cultural, and natural beauty of AlUla.

Guided Heritage Tours

Guided tours help visitors explore the historical landmarks of AlUla with the assistance of professional guides. These tours provide historical explanations about ancient civilizations and archaeological sites.

  • Hegra historical tours
  • Dadan archaeological tours
  • Old Town cultural tours

Transportation Services

Transportation services allow visitors to travel easily between the main attractions of AlUla. Organized transportation ensures that tourists can visit multiple locations comfortably.

  • Tour buses
  • Private transportation
  • Airport pickup services

Cultural Events

AlUla hosts a number of cultural festivals and international events that celebrate art, music, and heritage. These events attract visitors from around the world.

  • Winter at Tantora Festival
  • Art exhibitions
  • Traditional markets

Adventure Activities

For visitors seeking adventure, AlUla offers outdoor activities that allow them to explore the desert landscape and natural landmarks.

  1. Desert hiking
  2. Rock climbing
  3. Hot air balloon rides
  4. Desert camping

These tourism services help visitors enjoy a complete and memorable experience while exploring the rich heritage and natural beauty of AlUla.
